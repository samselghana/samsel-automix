    ✂️ Silence Trimmer

    Remove quiet sections from audio files — detects silence at the beginning and end, keeps the main content, exports _trimmed.mp3 (LAME in the browser). If MP3 encoding is unavailable, falls back to WAV.

    How to use: (1) Optional: quick export from the playlist (highlight a row, then use the button below). (2) Add files to the batch queue, set output (folder or downloads). (3) Choose Auto-detect (dB + padding + STFT, same idea as V3 PRO) or Fixed seconds. (4) Click Trim. (5) Read the trim log.
